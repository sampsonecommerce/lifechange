About Eva

My story started
in Stockholm.
It continues in Jaffa.

I am an experienced Organisational Change Consultant from Stockholm, Sweden, working since 1984 with individuals, groups and leaders through transformative processes. My own personal journey — including many travels and life in two reconstructed families — sparked a deep curiosity about cultures, family dynamics, and what makes real change possible.

To help others facing similar challenges, I deepened my practice with studies in Family Therapy and developed practical tools for navigating change within family complexity, reducing conflict and preventing emotional harm, especially for children.

In 2014, I followed a long-held dream and moved onto a boat in Jaffa, Israel. I continue my work here today — and online, across languages and continents.

Reconstructed Families

Building a new family
is rewarding — and
uniquely challenging.

I provide a safe, neutral space where every family member — adults and children alike — can express themselves and feel truly heard. With my own personal experience and training as a mediator, I can help you address conflicts early, sometimes even before they arise.

A unique part of my approach is that I sometimes include children directly in sessions. When parents find new partners, the biggest adjustments often fall on the children — giving them a voice makes all the difference.

Financial complexity Past investments, debts and commitments that shape the new family's freedom.
"My kids vs. your kids" The atmosphere of divided loyalty that quietly erodes a new family's foundation.
The emotional memory-pack Children carrying grief, feelings and loyalty toward the other parent — and what that means for everyone.
Balancing ex-partners and agreements Arrangements that limit the new couple's freedom and create ongoing friction.

How I Work

Every person is different.
Your solution should be too.

A customised plan, a holistic toolbox, and the belief that on average five focused sessions are enough to create meaningful, lasting change — though sometimes more are needed, and rarely less.

01

A Customised Plan

No two people share the same challenges or goals. I craft a plan designed specifically for your situation — and adapt it as we go. There is no one-size-fits-all approach here.

02

A Holistic Toolbox

Emotional insight, cognitive strategies and spiritual wisdom — woven together. My academic background spans Behavioural Sciences, Systemic Family Therapy, mediation, yoga and Healing Touch.

03

The Right Setting

Whenever possible, I recommend in-person sessions on the authentic, scenic port of Jaffa — far from any clinical feel. The setting is part of the work. Digital sessions are always an option.

A Unique Offering

Equine-Assisted
Relationship Coaching

One of my most powerful offerings involves specially trained horses. These sessions — conducted on the ground, together with a Certified Horse Therapist — integrate seamlessly into the broader therapeutic work. There is something uniquely honest about what happens when a horse enters the room. It is difficult to describe until you have experienced it.
